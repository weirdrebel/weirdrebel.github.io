Chicken

Chicken is a lean source of protein, rich in essential nutrients like vitamins B6 and B12, phosphorus, and selenium. Its low saturated fat content promotes heart health and aids muscle growth. Additionally, chicken offers versatility in cooking and can be a part of various balanced diets.

Mutton

Mutton, often derived from mature sheep, offers several benefits. It's a rich source of high-quality protein, essential vitamins like B12 and zinc, aiding muscle growth and immune function. Mutton also contains healthy fats, iron, and selenium, contributing to energy production and overall well-being.

Beef

Beef is a rich source of essential nutrients like protein, iron, zinc, and B vitamins. It supports muscle growth, immune function, and red blood cell production. However, moderation is key due to its saturated fat content, which can impact heart health if consumed excessively.
